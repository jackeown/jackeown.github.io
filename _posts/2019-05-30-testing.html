easyCanvas.js

Are you sick and tired of having to resize your canvas elements? Do you want an easy way to make plots using canvas? Do you have a hard time understanding how people use d3 so effectively, but still want a very general solution? If so, check out this project that I'm in the process of making...

It's meant to be a thin wrapper on top of html5 canvas. You give it the semantic dimensions of the canvas as "xmin", "xmax", "ymin" and "ymax" and set its visible dimensions using css.
